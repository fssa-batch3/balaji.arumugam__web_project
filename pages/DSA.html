Linked list

let us consider you are going to a movie with your friends(say 20 members).After you buy your tickets you notice none of the seats are contiguous.
But you feel you want to stay in touch with everyone in between the show time.(let's say during the songs).
But this is not easy since remembering everyone’s seat number is difficult.
So what would you do is,you would remember only one friend’s seat number and contact him , and he in turn remembers another’s and so on and the last one need not to remember any information(singly linked list) or the last person may remember first one’s(circular list).
In data structures,this concept is implemented using pointers/references.
Each node refers to some other node located at different address.
